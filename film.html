About the Film

Based on the Award-Winning Documentary

More than biography, this is a spiritual excavation — exploring how one man's relentless search for conscious contact transformed millions of lives, and continues to inspire seekers around the world.

Through rare archival material and deeply personal interviews, the film paints an intimate portrait of Bill Wilson's inner world: his struggles, his breakthroughs, and his unwavering commitment to spiritual growth.
