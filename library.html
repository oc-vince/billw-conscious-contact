About the Program

The Film & the Book, Together

An exploration of Bill Wilson's spiritual life and the philosophical roots of the Twelve Step Movement — produced in partnership with The Stepping Stones Foundation®. Winner of ten festival awards; screened internationally and across the U.S.

Twenty-five years of research into Bill Wilson's philosophy, his correspondence with Carl Jung, the influence of William James, and the Oxford Group — presented for general audiences, not just recovery specialists.

A natural fit for adult programming, recovery and wellness series, local history collections, and spiritual or philosophical discussion groups.
